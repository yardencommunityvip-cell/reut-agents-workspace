# Workflow: Weekly Community Insight & Action

## Step 1: Data Harvesting
- Access Google Sheets and WhatsApp logs (via connected tools).
- Identify the top 3 posts with the highest engagement (comments/likes).
- Measure "Member Growth" and "Active Participants".

## Step 2: Psychological Analysis
- Look beyond the numbers: *Why* did a post succeed? 
- Did it solve "Modern Loneliness"? Did it provide "Deep Talk"?
- Identify "Silent Members" who need a nudge to feel they belong.

## Step 3: Global Trend Matching
- Scan the sources in `B-brain/` for trending topics.
- Match a global trend to a local community need (e.g., "The new Bridgerton season is a perfect excuse for a local watch-party").

## Step 4: Executive Briefing (To Jordan)
- Tone: Energetic, "Let's do this now".
- Format: 
  1. What happened this week (The Numbers).
  2. Why it matters (The Insight).
  3. Action Plan: 3 concrete steps to increase belonging/engagement.
