# C-core Examples

These are **filled-in examples** showing what your C-core files should look like after you run the "Learn About Me" prompt.

Your actual C-core files start blank. These examples show you the finish line.

---

## Available Examples

| Example | Role | Best for |
|---------|------|----------|
| `example-freelancer.md` | Freelance Brand Designer | Freelancers, consultants, agency owners |
| `example-product-manager.md` | PM at a SaaS Company | Product managers, team leads, anyone in tech |
| `example-content-creator.md` | Newsletter Writer & Creator | Content creators, writers, marketers |

---

## How to Use These

1. **Read the example** closest to your role
2. **Notice the level of detail** - that's what your files should look like
3. **Fill in B-brain/01-about-me.md** with YOUR answers
4. **Run the Learn About Me prompt** to auto-fill your C-core files
5. **Compare** your filled files with the example. Similar depth? You're good.

---

## Key Patterns to Notice

- **project-brief.md** answers: Who are you? What problem do you solve? What makes you different?
- **icp-profile.md** answers: Who do you serve? What do they want? What's blocking them? Where do they hang out?
- **voice-dna.md** answers: How do you talk? What are you NOT? Can you show example sentences?

The more specific you are, the better your agents will perform.
